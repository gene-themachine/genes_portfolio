    Beat Machine

    The software enables users to utilize a streamlined digital audio workstation (DAW), with a primary focus on the trap genre.

    It features essential instruments such as bells, 808 bass, and drums. The core objective of this DAW is to facilitate music creation and provide a platform for users to learn the fundamentals of beat making.

    Change in Sonic Features of Viral Music

    During the COVID-19 pandemic, stay-at-home orders caused fear and anxiety related to social distancing and isolation. The digital age offers tools to analyze media consumption data, particularly music, which shapes moods and mental health during such times.

    This study examines changes in online music consumption behavior post-pandemic, finding increased 'acousticness' and decreased 'danceability' in Spotify tracks. These findings suggest using passive media data to monitor and predict population responses, aiding in policy-making and interventions.
